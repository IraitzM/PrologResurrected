# Logic Quest Product Overview

Logic Quest is an educational terminal-based game that teaches Prolog and logic programming concepts through an immersive 80s cyberpunk narrative.

## Core Concept
Players take on the role of a junior programmer at Cyberdyne Systems in 1985, tasked with repairing a malfunctioning AI research computer (LOGIC-1) by solving logical puzzles that teach fundamental Prolog concepts.

## Key Features
- **Retro Terminal Interface**: ASCII art, colored text, typewriter effects
- **Progressive Learning**: 5 levels teaching Facts, Rules, Unification, Backtracking, and Recursion
- **Interactive Storytelling**: Cyberpunk narrative woven into educational content
- **Puzzle-Based Learning**: Hands-on problem solving to reinforce concepts

## Target Audience
- Students learning Prolog and logic programming
- Developers interested in AI and logical reasoning
- Anyone wanting to understand the fundamentals of logic programming

## Educational Goals
- Teach core Prolog concepts through practical application
- Make abstract logical concepts concrete and engaging
- Provide immediate feedback and reinforcement
- Build understanding progressively from simple to complex concepts
